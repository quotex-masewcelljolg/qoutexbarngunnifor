## Developing a Trading Strategy

### Asset Selection

Choose assets with high trading volume and volatility that align with
your knowledge and understanding.

### Time Frame

Determine the expiration time for your trades based on your trading
style and analysis timeframe.

### Entry and Exit Points

Establish clear criteria for entering and exiting trades based on
technical indicators, chart patterns, or fundamental events.

### Risk Management

Set a maximum risk limit per trade (typically 1-2%) and use stop-loss
orders to protect your capital.

## Practical Examples of Trading Strategies

### Trend Following Strategy

Use moving averages to identify trend direction and trade in line with
the trend.

### Breakout Strategy

Identify support and resistance levels and enter trades when the price
breaks out of these levels.

### News Trading Strategy

Trade based on major news events by entering trades just before or after
significant announcements.
